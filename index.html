Our Mission

Multiversum is bridging the gap between technology and humanity. We create AI that listens, understands and speaks naturally, empowering enterprises to deliver truly personalised support at scale.

From prototype to production, we transform overloaded service desks into data-driven centres of excellence.

Sustainability Impact

By switching a typical 1 M request/day support workload from GPT-4 to Wonder.ai, power draw falls from ~1 000 kWh to 50 kWh, avoiding ≈ 380 kg CO₂ every day – and slashing cooling water use and hardware expansion. (Source: internal efficiency model)
